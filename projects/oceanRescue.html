Operation Ocean Rescue Game

Technologies Used: Unity | Blender | C# | Game Development

Category: Game Development | Educational Game | Environmental Awareness

Project Overview

Operation Ocean Rescue is an interactive game inspired by the narrative of The Deep Sea Mystery animation. The game transforms the story into a playable experience, where users take on the role of rescuing marine creatures affected by environmental issues. Through gameplay, users are encouraged to understand the importance of marine conservation. The project combines storytelling, game mechanics, and environmental themes to create an engaging and meaningful interactive experience.

My Role

As the team leader, I was responsible for planning the overall game concept and monitoring the project progress. I designed the gameplay concept and identified suitable actions, audio, and sound effects to enhance user experience. I also developed the tutorial scene to guide players and designed the game interface elements, including buttons and user interaction components.

Key Features

  • Interactive rescue-based gameplay
  • Story-driven environmental theme
  • Tutorial scene to guide new players
  • Integrated audio and sound effects for immersive experience

What I Learned

  • How to lead and coordinate a game development project
  • How to translate a story into interactive gameplay mechanics
  • The importance of audio and UI design in improving player experience
