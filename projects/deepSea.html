The Deep Sea Mystery 3D Animation

Technologies Used: Blender | 3D Animation

Category: 3D Animation | Storytelling | Multimedia Project

Project Overview

The Deep Sea Mystery is a 3D animation project centered on ocean inhabitants and their interaction with human activities. The story places ocean creatures as the key characters while humans serve as the main source of conflict within the narrative. This project emphasizes storytelling, visual communication, and animation techniques, while also highlighting environmental themes related to the underwater ecosystem. Blender was used to create the 3D models, scenes, and animation.

My Role

As the team leader, I managed task distribution and monitored the team’s progress throughout the project. I wrote the script, designed the sea turtle and fish citizen characters in both 2D and 3D, set up the scenes, and handled rendering for the animation. I also compiled and edited the rendered video clips from team members, then added subtitles and background music to produce the final animation.
